# Reporting & analytics

Track adoption without claiming clinical outcomes—focus on business indicators that show real progress.

## Who it's for

Execs and managers who need clear trending and accountability.

## What we deliver

- Trend views of key indicators by account/region
- Pipeline view of decision stages (interest → meeting → qualified → VAC → contract → first PO)
- Monthly read-outs and program adjustments

## Outcomes we target

Visibility across accounts • Earlier risk detection • Better conversion at each stage
